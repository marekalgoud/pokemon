Origin

Charmander is the basic Fire-type Starter Pokémon of Generation I, and is categorized within the Pokédex at #004 as the "Lizard Pokémon". It is known for the fire on the tip of its tail, which shows its vitality.

Death

If the tail were to go out, it would be the end of Charmander's life. Its tail is believed to emit steam when it rains. Charmander evolves into Charmeleon by leveling up.
